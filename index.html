OUR STORY

How we evolved to bring policy insights to life.

2023

Where It All Began

Our journey started at Georgia Tech as a class project, quickly evolving into a platform that transforms legislative and social media data into actionable insights.

2024

Developing our Framework

We combined NLP, network analysis, and data mining techniques to create tools mapping political discourse, sentiment, and influence. By leveraging
technologies like BERTopic, PolitiBERT, and Plotly.js, we built an interactive ecosystem for political and legislative analysis.

Today

Empowering Political Understanding

We provide a comprehensive view of political landscapes through state-level analysis of legislative data, social media conversations, and network connections.
Whether you are a voter, policymaker, or researcher, our platform delivers personalized insights into the intersection of policy and public sentiment.

The Future

Expanding Our Reach

We are committed to improving Gallop's capabilities by integrating more tools, and enhancing prediction models to stay ahead of the evolving political climate.
Our journey has just begun, and we are excited to bring transparency, clarity and insights into politics like never before.
